Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

Polymath LLC and its affiliated applications ("we," "us," or "our") are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our applications and services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Athlete profiles: name, sport, age or grade level (provided by parent or guardian)
  • Video recordings: sprint and training videos submitted for analysis
  • Analysis data: biomechanical assessments generated from submitted videos
  • Usage data: app interactions, session timestamps, and device information
  • Feedback: user-submitted comments, bug reports, and feature requests

How We Use Information

We use collected information to provide and improve our analysis services, generate personalized training recommendations, track athlete progress over time, respond to user feedback, and maintain the security of our systems. We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our applications may be used by athletes under the age of 13. We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13. Parents and guardians may review, modify, or request deletion of their child's information at any time by contacting us. We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services and do not serve targeted advertising to any users.

Data Storage and Security

Information is stored using industry-standard cloud infrastructure with encryption in transit and at rest. Video recordings are processed on secure servers and are not shared publicly. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and automated systems necessary for service delivery.

Data Retention and Deletion

Account data is retained while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Video recordings and analysis data can be individually deleted from within the application. Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You may export your data or request a copy at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through our applications or via email. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
